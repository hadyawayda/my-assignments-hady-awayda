History of Dekwaneh

Dekwaneh is a bustling suburb located in the eastern part of Beirut, Lebanon. With a rich history dating back to ancient times, it has evolved from a small village into a vibrant town.

Attractions

There are many attractions to see in Dekwaneh, including:

  • Dekwaneh Market: A bustling market offering a variety of local goods.
  • Saint Joseph Church: A beautiful church with stunning architecture.
  • Roud El Khachab Park: A lovely park perfect for a relaxing walk.

Culture

Dekwaneh boasts a rich cultural heritage with a blend of traditional and modern influences. The town is known for its festivals, local cuisine, and warm hospitality.
